Literacy is our favorite thing here at Read To Me!

Research shows us that a student's literacy level impacts their learning in all subjects. A key component of literacy is reading fluency.

Leveled Reading Texts

This website allows students to record up to 1 minute of themselves reading short texts organized by grade level.

Audio & Video Recording

By doing this, the student can play back their own recording to watch and hear how it sounds!

Gain Reading Confidence

This allows students to experience the text in a new way and gain confidence with text... a win-win!
